## System Overview

Shell manages a $25M+ educational investment portfolio through two
interconnected systems:

**CIS (Campus Investment System):** Manages annual campus funding
allocations. Campuses submit spend plans; Shell approves funding;
system tracks disbursements and carry-forwards year over year.

**Scholarships:** Manages scholarship applications, approvals, and
award tracking. Integrates with Shell's internal ISTS system for
post-award management. Includes an external public portal for
university student applications.
